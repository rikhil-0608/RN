About Wave Craze

Wave Craze is an electrifying festival celebrating music, entertainment, and creativity, organized by Radio NITroz, NIT Durgapur. Join us for an unforgettable experience!

Event Schedule

  • Day 1: Opening Ceremony , Movie mystic , Ramp Walk , Stand Up Comedy & DJ Night
  • Day 2: Talent show , Live Band Performances & Fun Activities

Events

TALENT X

In the hush of anticipation, where silence teems, 'Talent X 3.0' beckons, where stars are born. Radio NITroz, the official entertainment club of NIT Durgapur, presents Talent X 3.0 in collaboration with RED FM 93.5, an electrifying event where the spotlight shines on you and provides you with a chance to shine bright. Step onto our stage and let your talent dazzle our audience.

GLAM IT UP

Glamour is a gift of God, and with an eclectic mix of design and artistic expression, it becomes a spectacular art form. Glam it up—the ultimate thread that weaves through each segment, connecting the stages, the models, the designs, and the eager audience. Show your unmatched talent in the art of expression and style in WAVECRAZE with GLAM IT UP

DANCE Performances

Witness the rhythm, feel the beat, and experience the magic of movement as Dance Club takes center stage at the Wavecraze. Join us for an electrifying performance that transcends boundaries, blending various dance styles into a seamless spectacle of passion and precision. Get ready to be swept off your feet in a celebration of artistic expression – The Dance club awaits to ignite the dance floor and set the stage on fire!

MOVIE MYSTIC

Step into a world of cinematic excitement for an unforgettable evening filled with laughter, thrills, and the magic of the silver screen. COMEDY NIGHTS

Whether you're a fan of clever humor, observational comedy, or just love a good laugh, this event promises to leave you in stitches.
